Food Deserts in the United States


Purpose

The objective of this project is to focus attention to areas in the United States that lack access to affordable foods and fresh produce. The data will highlight which urban populations across the country are impacted the most in terms of easily-accessible foods. The location of the food deserts will also reveal how the lack of supermarkets correlate to areas of recorded low-income populations. I believe that this data will reveal just how big of an issue food deserts are in our own country.
